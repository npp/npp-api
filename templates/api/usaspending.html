Assistance Data

Federal assistance award data from the USASpending.gov archive files. Data are aggregated by program and geographic area.

Geography

County

Allowed keys: 'year', 'state_ansi', 'state_abbr', 'county_ansi', 'program_number'
Example: http://data.nationalpriorities.org/api/usaspending_assistance_county/list.json?state_abbr=FL&county_ansi=001&program_number=10.551&year=2011
