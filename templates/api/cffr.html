CFFR

URL Format:
http://data.nationalpriorities.org/api/<source>/list.<format>?...

Sources

There are two sources for CFFR data. They are

  • cffr
    Primary CFFR Data
    Allowed Keys: 'year', 'state_ansi', 'state_abbr', 'county_ansi', 'program_code', 'total'
    Totals are aggregated to the county level by default. Use 'total=state' to summarize by state.
    Example: http://data.nationalpriorities.org/api/cffr/list.json?state_abbr=LA&program_code=10.450&year=2010&total=state
  • cffrprogram
    CFFR Programs
    Allowed Keys: 'year', 'program_code', 'program_name'
    Example: http://data.nationalpriorities.org/api/cffrprogram/list.xml?year=2008
