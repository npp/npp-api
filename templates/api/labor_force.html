URL Format:

Sources

Available geography:

  • county
  • state

  • County Labor Force
    Annual Labor Force data by county, including employment and unemployment totals and unemployment rate.
    Allowed Keys: 'year', 'state', 'state_ansi', 'state_name', 'county_ansi'
    Example: http://data.nationalpriorities.org/api/labor_force_county/list.json?state=nv
  • State Labor Force
    Annual Labor Force data by state, including employment and unemployment totals, unemployment rate, employment population ratio, and labor force participation rate.
    Allowed Keys: 'year', 'state', 'state_ansi', 'state_name'
    Example: http://data.nationalpriorities.org/api/labor_force_state/list.json?state=mt&year=2011
