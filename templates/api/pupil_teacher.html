Pupil/Teacher Ratio

URL Format:
http://data.nationalpriorities.org/api/<source>/list.<format>?...

Sources

Available geography:

  • school district
  • state

  • School District Pupil/Teacher Ratio
    Number of students for every public school teacher. Year shown reflects end of the school year.
    Allowed Keys: 'state', 'year', 'agency_id', 'agency_name'
    Example: http://data.nationalpriorities.org/api/pupil_teacher_district/list.json?state=MN&year=2007
  • State Pupil/Teacher Ratio
    Number of students for every public school teacher. Year shown reflects end of the school year.
    Allowed Keys: 'year', 'state', 'state_ansi'
    Example: http://data.nationalpriorities.org/api/pupil_teacher_state/list.xml?year=2009
